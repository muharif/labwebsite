Precision Network Lab

From molecular networks to clinical decisions: advancing systems medicine for progressive diseases.

Network Biology

Unraveling molecular networks to understand disease mechanisms

AI-Driven Discovery

Machine Learning & Multi-omics for disease diagnosis and drug discovery

Research Focus

🔗 Network-Based Multi-Omics Integration

We develop network-based methods to integrate multi-omics data, uncovering complex molecular interactions across biological layers.

🧩 Multi-Tissue Crosstalk

Our work maps inter-tissue communication in cardiometabolic disease, connecting signals from liver, muscle, adipose, heart, and gut microbiome.

⏳ Time-Dynamic Disease Progression

We analyze disease trajectories over time to identify stage-specific biomarkers and therapeutic targets.

🤖 AI-Driven In-Silico Models

We build predictive, AI-powered models to simulate disease mechanisms and guide personalized treatment strategies.
